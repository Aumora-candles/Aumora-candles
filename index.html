Welcome to Aumora Candles

Hi there! We're so glad you're here. At Aumora Candles, we believe that the right candle can transform any space — turning an ordinary moment into something warm, memorable, and entirely your own.

Every candle we make is 100% handmade, carefully crafted in small batches to ensure quality you can see, smell, and feel. From the wax to the wick, each piece is poured with intention — no two are ever quite the same.

✨ Unique designs

No mass production — every candle is one of a kind

🌸 Rich scents

Carefully blended fragrances for every mood

🤍 Made with love

Handpoured with care, from our hands to your home

Aumora Candles was born from a simple idea: that beautiful, high-quality candles shouldn't have to come from a big brand. Our little company was started by two teenagers with a shared passion for creativity, fragrance, and making something truly special.

What began as a hobby quickly turned into something we love sharing with the world.

We're just getting started — and we can't wait for you to find your perfect flame. 🕯️
